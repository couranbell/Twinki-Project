BookRat - Independent Bookshop in London

A special spot where you can enjoy the perfect combination of a wide selection of books, a close-knit community, and great fun! Take a look at our bookstore's landing page to get to know us better.

Who knows - maybe you're the next bookrat?

We are a relatively young and independent bookstore with a strong ambition to unite people in these challenging times. Despite that, of course, we offer a wide collection of books: from romance and business to crime fiction and poetry, from memoirs to classic literature, and more. So you can be sure that you won't leave empty-handed.

However, visiting us just to drop by and ask "what's up" or how we are doing is also worth it. Book presentations, author meetings, and book club events take place regularly, so you can join our community and make reading a meaningful part of your life! And that is exactly our aim!
